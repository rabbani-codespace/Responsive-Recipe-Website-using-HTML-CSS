Gulam Jaamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder
  • Maida (all-purpose flour)
  • Sugar syrup

Recipie

  1. Mix milk maida with water to make soft dough.
  2. Make small round balls.
  3. Fry balls in oil on low flame until golden brown.
  4. Dip in sugar syrup and rest for 30 minutes.

Barfie Jaamun

Ingredients

  • khoya (mawa)
  • Maida
  • Sugar syrup

Recipie

  1. Mix khoya and maida to make smooth dough.
  2. Shape into small balls.
  3. Fry in oil until golden color.
  4. Dip in sugar syrup and garnish.

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Boil milk and add lemon juice to make paneer.
  2. Drain water and knead paneer into soft dough.
  3. Make small balls and boil in sugar syrup.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes and serve fresh.
